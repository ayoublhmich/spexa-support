Getting started

What can I do in Spexa?
Spexa has two main parts:
  • Structured lessons — 40 lessons across 8 sections covering greetings, introductions, work, travel, daily life, social situations, culture, and advanced topics. Each lesson mixes vocabulary cards, multiple-choice questions, listening exercises, speaking practice, and pronunciation scoring.
  • AI voice tutor — open-ended conversation practice. Tap the microphone, speak naturally, and the AI tutor replies with voice, corrects your grammar, and suggests better phrasings.
You can switch between the two whenever you want.
How does Spexa work?
Spexa uses advanced AI to transcribe your speech, score your pronunciation, and generate spoken tutor responses in real time. In lessons, you progress through exercises and earn streaks for practicing daily. With the AI tutor, you can have a free-form conversation and get instant feedback on grammar, pronunciation, and word choice.
What languages can I learn?
The structured lesson curriculum is currently focused on English. The AI tutor also supports conversational practice in the target language you pick during onboarding — so if you chose Spanish, for example, the tutor will speak to you in Spanish. You can change your target language at any time from your profile.
Do I need an internet connection?
Yes. Spexa needs an active internet connection for speech recognition, pronunciation scoring, and AI tutor responses. A slow or unstable connection may cause delays or failed recordings.
What level do I need?
Spexa works for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced learners. You pick your level during onboarding, and the content — as well as the AI tutor's speaking style and complexity — adapts to it. You can raise or lower your level later from your profile.
How do I start a lesson?
Open the Journey tab, pick an unlocked lesson tile, and follow the steps. Most lessons take 3–6 minutes. Your progress is saved automatically, so you can pause mid-lesson and resume later.
How do I talk to the AI tutor?
Open the Home tab, tap the gold microphone button, and speak naturally. Release when you're done — the tutor will reply with voice and a text bubble. You can also tap the keyboard icon to switch to typed input, the lamp icon for a hint if you don't know what to say, and the play button on any message to hear it again.
Why do my lessons mention my country or city?
We automatically adapt example names, cities, and country references inside lessons to match your region, so the content feels culturally relevant. The region is inferred from your device's language and country settings — no GPS or precise location is used. If the region feels wrong, you can change your device's region settings and the next lesson you open will reflect the change.
Can I track my progress?
Yes. Spexa tracks your daily streak, completed lessons, total speaking time, listening time, vocabulary learned, and pronunciation-scoring results. You can see all of it in your profile and the Insights tab. Missing a day pauses your streak — pick it back up by completing any lesson or AI tutor session.

Subscriptions & Billing

How much does Spexa cost?
Spexa offers weekly, monthly, and yearly subscription plans. Current prices are shown inside the app and on the App Store / Google Play listing, and may vary by country. A free trial may be available for new users at the plan you choose.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Because subscriptions are managed by the app store you purchased from, you cancel there — not inside Spexa. Uninstalling the app does NOT cancel a subscription.

On iPhone / iPad (Apple):

  • Open the Settings app and tap your name at the top.
  • Tap "Subscriptions".
  • Select Spexa.
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription" at least 24 hours before the next billing date.

On Android (Google Play):

  • Open the Google Play Store app.
  • Tap your profile icon, then "Payments & subscriptions" → "Subscriptions".
  • Select Spexa.
  • Tap "Cancel subscription" and follow the prompts at least 24 hours before the next billing date.
More help: Apple · Google Play.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by the app store, not by Spexa directly.
  • Apple: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your Spexa purchase, and request a refund. See Apple's refund policy here.
  • Google Play: open the Google Play Store, go to your order history, find the Spexa purchase, and request a refund. See Google's refund policy here.
If you think you were charged in error, email support@spexa.space and we'll help you navigate the right process.
When will I be charged?
You'll be charged at the end of any free trial period, and then at the start of each subscription period (weekly, monthly, or yearly, depending on the plan you chose). Charges appear on the payment method associated with your Apple ID or Google account. Renewal happens automatically within 24 hours before the current period ends unless you cancel.

Technical Support

The app isn't recognizing my voice. What should I do?
  • Check microphone permission. On iPhone: Settings → Spexa → Microphone (enabled). On Android: Settings → Apps → Spexa → Permissions → Microphone (allowed).
  • Make sure your device isn't in silent or Do Not Disturb mode, and that no other app (call, voice recorder, etc.) is using the microphone at the same time.
  • Speak clearly and keep background noise low — loud cafés, fans, or open windows can confuse the speech recognizer.
  • Check that you're connected to the internet — speech recognition runs in the cloud.
  • Close and reopen the app, then try again. If it still fails, restart your device.
If the issue persists, contact us at support@spexa.space with your device model and OS version.
The app is crashing, freezing, or audio is cutting out
  • Make sure you have the latest version of Spexa from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Update your device to the latest iOS / Android version.
  • Close other apps running in the background.
  • If audio playback is choppy, check your Wi-Fi or cellular signal — slow networks cause the tutor's voice response to buffer.
  • As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app. Your progress, streak, and subscription are tied to your account, not your device, so they'll return when you sign back in.
Still having issues? Email support@spexa.space with your device model, OS version, and a short description of what happened. Our crash-reporting tools also help us spot problems automatically.
I can't log in to my account
  • Make sure you're signing in with the same method you originally used — Apple ID, Google account, or email/password.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If you used "Hide My Email" when signing in with Apple, keep using the same Apple ID — the hidden-relay address is what's linked to your account.
  • Try signing out and back in.
Contact support@spexa.space if you still can't access your account, and include the email address associated with it.
My lesson progress or streak looks wrong
Your progress is synced to your account in real time while you have internet. If something looks off:
  • Make sure the device is online and fully close / reopen the app to trigger a refresh.
  • Check that you're signed in with the correct account — progress is tied to the account, not the device.
If the problem persists, email support@spexa.space with your account email and what you expected to see vs. what's showing.

Account & Privacy

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account and its associated data in two ways:
  • Inside the app: open the More tab → Settings → Delete Account, and follow the confirmation prompts.
  • By email: send a message to privacy@spexa.space with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from the email address on your account.
We process deletion requests within 30 days. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone, and that cancelling an active subscription must be done separately through the App Store or Google Play.
What data does Spexa collect?
We collect what's needed to provide and improve the Service:
  • Profile: name, email, target language, native language, proficiency level, goals, and interests you provide at onboarding.
  • Device: device type, OS version, language settings, country code inferred from your device locale, and crash / error reports.
  • Usage: lesson progress, streak, exercise results, and how you move through the app.
  • Voice and text: your spoken audio and typed messages are sent to our AI providers to transcribe, score, and generate responses. We do not permanently store raw audio on our servers.
  • Subscription: transaction identifiers and renewal status from Apple or Google.
We do not sell your data. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
Is my voice data secure?
Yes. Voice recordings are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and processed only to give you a transcription, pronunciation score, or tutor response. We don't persist the raw audio files on our own servers once the result is returned. Our AI partners (see the Privacy Policy) may retain requests per their own policies, under industry-standard security. We never use your voice for advertising.
What AI services does Spexa use?
Spexa uses Google Gemini for speech recognition, conversation, and pronunciation scoring, and OpenAI for text-to-speech and supporting language features. These services run entirely in the cloud. If you're curious about exactly what each provider receives, it's all listed in Section 5 of our Privacy Policy.
Can I change my country, target language, or level later?
Yes. Your target language, native language, proficiency level, learning goals, and interests can all be updated from your profile at any time. Country is inferred from your device's region settings; changing your device region and reopening Spexa will update the country the app uses for content personalization.
